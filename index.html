Urban Sketchers Paphos

Community Growth Case

Overview

Urban Sketchers Paphos is a local chapter of a global community, built from zero.

This case explores how community identity, engagement, and growth were developed through structured marketing and content.

Role

  • Founded and built the community from scratch
  • Developed brand identity
  • Designed engagement system
  • Organized events and exhibitions
  • Managed partnerships and visibility

Growth Strategy

Starting point: no audience, no visibility.

Approach:

  • Consistent local presence
  • Events as core growth driver
  • Strong visual identity
  • Integration into global network

Growth loop:

Event → content → visibility → new participants → next event

Results

  • 10× community growth in the first year
  • 300+ exhibition visitors
  • Local media coverage
  • Integration into global network

Key Insight

People don’t join communities for content. They join for identity and shared experience.
